Allens

Case Study

Challenge

Founded in 1822, its alumni include a former Australian Prime Minister, High and Supreme Court justices, a former President of the World Bank, and many ASX executives.

With over 1,200 lawyers supporting clients across a dozen sectors, how does an organisation with such depth and breadth of experience communicate this value in a tangible way for clients and prospects?

Solution

Allens uses video extensively for marketing and business development, sharing their experience in a dynamic format for competitive advantage.

Allens On Air is a branded video gallery

“People are increasingly wanting to do more sophisticated things with video. We’ve never had an issue where we thought we want to do something and there’s not that tool… or the answer at the end of the phone.”

David Bradbury, Web & Extranets Manager - Allens Linklaters
