QBE

Case Study

Challenge

As one of the 20 largest ASX listed companies in Australia, QBE has a diverse base of sophisticated analysts and investors around the world. With a complex business portfolio operating in multiple global markets, QBE has a need to reach and engage it’s investors around the world with operational and financial updates.

Solution

QBE provides regular investor briefings through interactive live streams, enabling the CEO, CFO and executive team to communicate to thousands of investors, anywhere in the world, at the same time.

And, as one of the most innovative Investor Relations teams in the country, QBE has developed a native iOS and Android App that provides a record of all these briefings that can be accessed by the global financial community on any device, from anywhere.

Jeva Centeno, Investor Relations Manager - QBE
