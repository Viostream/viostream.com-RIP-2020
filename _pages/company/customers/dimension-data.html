Dimension Data

Case Study

Challenge

As one of the largest global technology solution providers, Dimension Data is also an innovator, and recognised early on that streaming video would help them connect and engage their people in the many worldwide regions in which they operate.

Solution

DD TV, a secure, branded video gallery, is a hub for video communications in each region to share business updates, and content for organisational development and training.
