We're committed to your success

The purpose of Viostream's leadership team is to make directional and governance decisions that maximise the success of the business, our people and our customers.

They're just the tip of a team of incredibly talented and passionate people who are building a kick-ass video solution for our customers and helping them get maximum value from it.

Executive team

Neil Jackson
CEO

Neil leads the Executive team at Viostream, and is a member of the Board of Directors. 25 years+ sales management experience working with Unisys, Microsoft and AWS in Australia and overseas.

Avi Lewis
Head of Finance and People

Avi leads the finance, legal and HR function at Viostream. He joined Viostream in January 2019 and has over 25 years experience in various senior finance positions with global companies such as Citrix, Yodlee, ANZ Bank and Firstwave Cloud Technology. He is a qualified CPA, and holds an MBA(Technology) degree from the AGSM UNSW. Avi loves spending time outdoors whether its on a beach, on a boat or in the bush, and love his jazz, blues, funk and classic rock music.

Paul Vecchiato
Chief Technology Officer

Paul is responsible for leading the technology function at Viostream, ensuring our customers have uninterrupted access to a reliable, secure and effective product. This incorporates infrastructure, software development and dev ops. Paul is an avid Sydney FC supporter.
