Suncorp

Case Study

Challenge

With dozens of brands and thousands of staff across hundreds of sites, Suncorp needed a way to bring together and engage their people in a more dynamic way than their SharePoint intranet site could deliver.

Solution

Suncorp TV is a secure video gallery that acts as a hub for executive messages, business updates, training and knowledge sharing. More than a thousand videos are categorised by brand or region, with a search function to surface the most relevant content. And to reinforce the fact that Suncorp TV is a social video platform, they launched with a user generated content campaign that generated content and engagement from right across the organisation.

“Over 1,000 videos were uploaded to Suncorp TV in the first 12 months”
