John Holland

Case Study

Challenge

John Holland is a people business. And with more than 4,000 people spread across hundreds of sites around the world, they needed an innovative way to focus on health and safety messages to underpin the culture of people protecting people that is central to their business philosopy.

Solution

John Holland TV is a secure, responsive video site that employees can access on their computers, tablets or mobile devices wherever they may be. It’s a way for the business leaders to share important messages in a human way, and for workers in the field to record and share their own updates, initiatives and project milestones.

“To share that story… shares inspiration, capability, innovation and especially people”

Paul Gaudion, ICT Mobility Program Manager - John Holland
