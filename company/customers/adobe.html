Adobe

Case Study

Challenge

As one of the leading brands in the world of content creation, marketing and digital communication, Adobe’s signature Symposium and MakeIt are invariably oversubscribed, despite occupying the largest venues available. These events feature many high profile speakers whose content can inspire people well beyond the event date. So how do they maximise reach and engagement not just on the day of the conference, but in the months and years beyond?

Solution

Adobe relies on Viostream to broadcast their conference sessions live online, and to capture these for on-demand viewing. This enables people to access the conference in real-time from their computer or mobile device, or to join in at one of dozens of satellite events. And with on-demand recordings available online immediately afterwards, the value of the content is maximised by reaching a limitless audience.
